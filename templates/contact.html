About Carbon Crush

Carbon Crush is an App designed by Green Queens to help individuals understand their own impact on the environment.

Integrated with built-in leader-board and carbon calculator, Carbon Crush tracks progress and makes recommendations to off-set activities to make small life-style changes to support a sustainable world.

Connect with your colleagues, build a team, or register as an individual.
