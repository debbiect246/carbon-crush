Myth VS. Science

Myth 1.
"The climate has changed before so we do not need to worry"

Yes, the climate has hugely changed over the course of it’s 4.5 billion year existence.

However, the climate responds to whatever forces it to change at the time, and currently humans are the dominant influence.

Myth 2.
"Climate change is a future problem"

The effects of climate change are already being seen today.

Its impact is already having an adverse affect on the oceans, human health, agriculture, sea level, biodiversity and the economy.

Myth 3.
"Climate models are unreliable"

Climate models are mathematical representations of major drivers of climate, including the atmosphere, oceans, land and ice.

Many models have accurately projected changes in global surface temperature.
